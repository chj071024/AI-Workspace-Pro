# Project Name

Personal Brand Website

---

# Project Purpose

This project is my official personal brand website.

Its primary purpose is to introduce who I am, showcase my capabilities, build trust, and convert visitors into potential clients.

This website is not built for entertainment.

It is not a blog.

It is not a portfolio gallery.

It is a business website whose main objective is to generate qualified leads.

Every design, feature, and content decision should support this objective.

---

# About Me

I am an independent developer.

I build practical digital solutions for individuals, creators, freelancers, and small businesses.

I focus on solving real business problems instead of simply delivering code.

I value communication, reliability, simplicity, and long-term maintainability.

# Target Clients

The website is primarily designed for:

- Small businesses

What services do I provide?
